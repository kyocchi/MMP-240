How to Make Limoncello

This is the best lemoncello recipe ever! Simple ingredients, quick to prepaer and amazingly delicious.
This lemoncello turns out perfectly every single time.

Ingredients: unit: g(but not lb) FYI 1000g=1kg

  • Organic lemons 40g
  • Vodka (Everclear 95% ) 100g(=126cc=126ml)
  • Granulated sugar 75g
  • Leakwam Water 150g

Instructions

  1. After wash 3 lemons, peel a skin of the lemons. Don't use white part, but only yellow part called zest.
  2. Place an empty bottle or container on the scale and set it to 0g.
  3. Put all of zest into the bottle or container and scale.
  4. If lemon zest is 44g, which is 10% more than 40g, so vodka is also 110g, which is 10% more.
  5. Then you have to cover the bottle or container with aluminum foil to keep it out of the light, or keep it in total darkness place where is no light for 2 days.
  6. Make syrup with 150g of lukewarm water and 75g of granulated sugar. Mix well to completely dissolve the sugar.
  7. Pour the jar or container that has been left in a dark place for 2 days into the above 6 while straining the lemon zest with a tea strainer.
  8. Milky white Lemon cello is ready. If you put it in the refrigerator for a week, the ingredients will be more familiar and it will be more delicious.
