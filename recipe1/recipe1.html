How to Make Limoncello

This is the best limoncello recipe ever!
Simple ingredients, quick to prepaer and amazingly delicious.
This limoncello turns out perfectly every single time.

Ingredients: unit g (NOT lb) FYI 1000g=1kg

  • Organic lemon zest 40g
  • Vodka (Everclear 95% ) 100g (=126cc=126ml)
  • Granulated sugar 75g
  • Leakwam Water 150g

Instructions

  1. Wash 3 lemons and peel the skin off each lemon.
  2. Use only the yellow part of the skin, which is called zest. Avoid using the white part.
  3. Place an empty bottle or container on a scale and set it to zero grams.
  4. Add all of the lemon zest to the bottle or container, and weigh it.
  5. If the weight of the lemon zest is 44g, then it is 10% more than 40g. Thus, add 10% more vodka, which is 110g.
  6. Cover the bottle or container with aluminum foil to keep it away from light. Alternatively, keep it in a dark place without any light for 2 days.
  7. Prepare the syrup by mixing 150g of lukewarm water with 75g of granulated sugar. Mix well until the sugar dissolves completely.
  8. After 2 days, strain the lemon zest using a tea strainer and pour the contents of the bottle or container into the syrup mixture.
  9. The milky white lemon cello is now ready. If you refrigerate it for a week, the flavors will blend more, resulting in a more delicious drink.
